Beginner Workout Plan

  • Bodyweight Squats

    Description: Bodyweight squats are great for building strength in the lower body, especially your quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes.

  • Push-Ups

    Description: Push-ups strengthen your chest, shoulders, triceps, and core.

  • Plank

    Description: The plank is an excellent core-strengthening exercise.

  • Lunges

    Description: Lunges target the quadriceps, glutes, and hamstrings, improving balance and coordination.

  • Dumbbell Rows

    Description: Dumbbell rows build strength in the upper back, shoulders, and biceps.

  • Glute Bridge

    Description: The glute bridge works the glutes, hamstrings, and lower back.

  • Standing Overhead Dumbbell Press

    Description: This exercise targets your shoulders, triceps, and upper chest.
