Intermediate Workout Plan

  • Barbell Deadlifts

    Description: Deadlifts are a total-body exercise targeting the back, legs, and core. Ideal for intermediate strength building.

  • Barbell Bench Press

    Description: The bench press focuses on building chest, shoulder, and tricep strength.

  • Bent Over Rows

    Description: This exercise targets the back muscles and biceps, improving upper-body strength and posture.

  • Bulgarian Split Squats

    Description: Bulgarian split squats are perfect for developing unilateral leg strength and balance.

  • Pull-Ups

    Description: Pull-ups are excellent for building upper back and biceps strength, while also engaging the core.

  • Dips

    Description: Dips are a great bodyweight exercise for developing chest and tricep strength.

  • Romanian Deadlifts

    Description: Romanian deadlifts target the hamstrings and lower back, ideal for improving posterior chain strength.
