Top Beginner Workout Plan

  • Jumping Jacks

    Description: Jumping jacks are a great cardio exercise that increases heart rate, burns calories, and improves stamina.

  • Wall Sit

    Description: Wall sits strengthen the quadriceps and engage the core while enhancing endurance.

  • Arm Circles

    Description: Arm circles are a simple and effective way to warm up the shoulders and upper arms.

  • Modified Push-Ups

    Description: A beginner-friendly version of push-ups that build chest, shoulders, and triceps strength.

  • High Knees

    Description: High knees are a cardio exercise that engages the core and improves balance and coordination.

  • Mountain Climbers

    Description: Mountain climbers work the entire body, focusing on the core, legs, and upper body.

  • Leg Raises

    Description: Leg raises target the lower abs and help strengthen the core.
